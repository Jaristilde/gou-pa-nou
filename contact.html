Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

Where can I buy GOU PA NOU seasonings?

Our seasonings are available on Etsy and Amazon. Visit our Shop page for direct links to purchase.

Do you offer wholesale pricing?

Yes! We work with restaurants, specialty food stores, and other retailers. Please send us a message with your business details for wholesale inquiries.

Are your seasonings gluten-free?

Most of our seasonings are made with naturally gluten-free ingredients. Please check individual product descriptions for specific allergen information.

How should I store my seasonings?

Store in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight. Keep the cap tightly sealed to maintain freshness and flavor.

Do you ship internationally?

Shipping options vary by platform. Please check our Etsy or Amazon stores for current shipping availability to your location.
